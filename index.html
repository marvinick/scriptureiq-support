Q: How do I sync my progress across devices?
A: Create an account by tapping "Login" in the Settings tab. Your progress, streaks, and achievements will automatically sync when you log in on any device.
Q: Can I use the app without an internet connection?
A: Yes! Download questions in the Settings tab for offline use. You can answer questions offline, and your progress will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
Q: How do the challenge modes work?
A: Complete 10 questions in Quiz mode to unlock challenges like Lightning Gauntlet, Speed Round, and Streak Master. Each challenge tests different skills and awards achievement badges.
Q: What is the Holy Fire feature?
A: The Holy Fire grows brighter with consecutive correct answers. Build your fire by maintaining accuracy and watch it evolve through different stages!
Q: How do I reset my progress for a specific category?
A: Go to Stats > Score tab and find the category you want to reset. Tap the "‚Üª Reset" button next to that category.
Q: Why do I see different question counts in different places?
A: The hero images show your locally downloaded questions, while some stats may reflect the total available on our servers. Download the latest questions in Settings for the most current content.

üîß Troubleshooting

  • App crashes or freezes: Close and restart the app. If issues persist, restart your device.
  • Questions not loading: Check your internet connection and try downloading questions in Settings.
  • Progress not syncing: Ensure you're logged in and have an internet connection. Try logging out and back in.
  • Login issues: Check your email and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.
  • Missing achievements: Achievements may take a moment to appear. Try switching tabs or restarting the app.
  • Audio or visual issues: Check your device's volume and display settings.
